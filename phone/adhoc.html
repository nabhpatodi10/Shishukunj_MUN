A Message from the Vice Chairperson

Namaste everyone,

I, Manas Pandey, will be serving as the vice- chair of the AD- HOC committee for the 2021 Shishukunj MUN.

I still remember my first MUN. It was very difficult to comprehend anything the committee was talking about. I was exposed to a unique spectrum of debates, socialising, and public speaking. Since that day I have participated in more than six MUN from all over India, and I assure you, the experience was enriching.

I am looking forward to meeting you all and listening to your substantive materials, and to enjoy the three days we will be together.

Regards

Manas Pandey

Vice-Chairperson, AdHoc

 

Organ Description

AdHoc is a temporary committee called upon to address a specific issue and is dissolved after the completion of the task. The nature of the committee will require quick thinking, farsight and deep knowledge of the agenda.

 

NOTE: Agenda and all other information regarding the committee will be revealed 48 hours prior to the Conference.

 

Note: Incase of any queries, you can contact the Bureau at adhoc@shishukunj.in
