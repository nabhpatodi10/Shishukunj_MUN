About Us

Shishukunj MUN

Welcome to the 6th iteration of the Shishukunj Model United Nations Conference. Shishukunj MUN was started with the aim of encouraging an all-round understanding of the United Nations and stimulating young minds to think about previous as well as prevailing global issues, and over the years it has been quite successful in providing the delegates with a widespread world-view. Carrying the legacy forward, we have Shishukunj MUN 2021, a two day conference scheduled from October 10 - October 11, 2021. From heated discussions in GA committees to continuous crises in specialized committees to newsletter and pictures, it is sure to be an exciting two days! The theme for the conference this year is-Coordination, Contemplation, Commonalities Keeping in mind the extreme pandemic situation we went through in the past one and a half year we felt the need for nations to cooperate and coordinate in order to bring the economies and lives of the world back to the normal. And seeing the humanitarian, economic, power dominance based political issues we have a lot to contemplate and think about.

Our TEAM (Together We Achieve More) A dedicated secretariat, enthusiastic and capable bureau, and a well-equipped organizing committee together are sure to provide an enriching learning experience to both our old and new delegates.

A Platform for Insightful Discussions and Debates This year the Shishukunj MUN features 10 different committees each having a unique and very interesting agenda of its own. The study guides will be intricately created by the bureau members and will give a systematic view of the agenda in hand while also stimulating the delegates to think beyond it. Also this being an Intra school MUN, delegates will be able to lose their hesitation and learn to be more confident in their stance, especially the first timers.
